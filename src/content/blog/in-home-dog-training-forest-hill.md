## What private in-home training looks like

A senior K9 Academy trainer comes to your Forest Hill home. We assess your dog in your actual environment and build a plan around the specific issues you're dealing with.

Common issues we address in Forest Hill homes:

- **Guest reactivity.** Your dog loses it when someone comes through the front door. We train at your front door.
- **Leash pulling on Spadina and St. Clair.** Busy streets, other dogs, squirrels in the park. We train on your actual walking route.
- **Separation anxiety.** Large Forest Hill homes give anxious dogs more room to panic. We work on this in your specific space.
- **Off-leash recall.** Cedarvale Park and the Beltline are incredible for off-leash dogs. But only if your recall is reliable.
