## Why it works better than facility training

Dogs are context learners. They associate commands with the place they learned them. A dog that recalls perfectly at a training facility might completely ignore you on the Rosedale ravine trail.

In-home training eliminates this problem. Your dog learns where it needs to perform.

This is especially important for:

- **Doorbell and guest reactivity.** Rosedale homes have a lot of foot traffic, deliveries, and visitors. We train your dog to handle all of it calmly.
- **Off-leash reliability.** The ravine system is incredible for off-leash walks, but only if your dog's recall is bulletproof.
- **Separation anxiety.** Large homes can actually make separation anxiety worse. Your dog has more space to pace, destroy, and panic.
