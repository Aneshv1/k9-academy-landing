A reactive dog success story

A client came to us last year with a 3-year-old Lab mix who was so reactive on leash that walks had become a source of dread. She'd lunge, bark, and spin at the sight of any dog within 50 feet. The owner had started driving to empty parks at 6 AM just to avoid other dogs.

After 6 private training sessions over 8 weeks, this dog now walks through Leaside on a loose leash. Past dogs, past squirrels, past cyclists. The owner told us: "I actually enjoy walking my dog now. I forgot what that felt like."

Reactivity is one of the most common issues we treat — and one of the most fixable.

If you're crossing the street to avoid other dogs, we can help.
