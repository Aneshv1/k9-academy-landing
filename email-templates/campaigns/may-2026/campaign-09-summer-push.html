Patios, parks, and guests — is your dog ready?

Summer in Toronto means patios, parks, cottage weekends, backyard barbecues, and visitors.

It also means your dog will be tested in ways they aren't during the quieter months:

Can they settle at a patio while you eat?

Can they walk past other dogs at Trinity Bellwoods without losing it?

Can they greet your guests without jumping?

Can they be off leash at the cottage?

If the answer to any of those is "no" — now is the time to fix it. Not in August when the summer is half over.

Our group classes, private training, and Board & Train all have spots available right now. By July, your dog could be a completely different animal.
