A lot of Board & Train programs are essentially boarding with 20 minutes of training tacked on. That's not what we do.

Here's what a typical day looks like for your dog at K9 Academy:

6:30 AM — Wake up, outdoor time, structured walk

8:00 AM — Obedience session #1

Leash work, commands, impulse control

10:00 AM — Socialization or environmental exposure

New sounds, surfaces, dogs

12:00 PM — Rest and crate time

Crate training is built in

2:00 PM — Obedience session #2

Distraction proofing, real-world scenarios

4:00 PM — Play, decompression, unstructured time

6:00 PM — Evening walk, settling practice

8:00 PM — Wind down, crate

Your dog gets 3–4 hours of structured training every day. Not 20 minutes. That's why our results stick.

We also send video updates so you can see your dog's progress in real time.
