**Subject:** The malinois that almost got put down

**Preview:** (leave blank for plain-text)

---

Three years ago I had a client with a Belgian Malinois that had already bitten two people.

Every trainer they'd seen told them the dog was too dangerous. One vet suggested euthanasia.

We spent 6 weeks together. I won't pretend it was easy. The first two weeks were the hardest board and train I've ever done.

But that dog now lives with two kids. Off leash. Calm. Happy.

The dog wasn't broken. The approach was.

I think about that case a lot because it reminds me why we do what we do. Most trainers would have turned that dog away. We didn't.

If you're dealing with something similar and have been told there's no hope, reply to this email. I read every one.
