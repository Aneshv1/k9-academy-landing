**Subject:** Board & Train vs Private Lessons. Which one is right?

**Preview:** Honest breakdown. No upselling.

---

This is the most common question we get. Here's the honest answer.

## Choose Board & Train if:
- Your dog's issues are moderate to severe (aggression, reactivity, anxiety)
- You don't have time to train consistently yourself
- You want the fastest possible results
- You've already tried other trainers
- Your dog needs a full behavioural reset

**Cost:** $2,995 (2wk) to $4,995 (6wk)
**Timeline:** Results in 2-6 weeks

## Choose Private Lessons if:
- You want to learn alongside your dog
- Your dog's issues are specific (leash pulling, recall, settling)
- You can commit to weekly sessions and practice between them
- Budget is a factor

**Cost:** $1,350 (6 sessions) or $1,685 (8 sessions)
**Timeline:** Results over 6-8 weeks

## The Hybrid Approach
Some clients do Board & Train first to build the foundation, then add private lessons to fine-tune specific things at home. This combination works especially well for reactive dogs.
