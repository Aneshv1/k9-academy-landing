**Subject:** What I've learned training 10,000 dogs

**Preview:** (leave blank for plain-text)

---

I've been doing this for 15 years. Over 10,000 dogs.

Here are the three things I know for sure:

1. The dog is almost never the problem. The owner's approach is. Change the approach and the dog changes overnight. I've seen it thousands of times.

2. Every dog can be fixed. Every single one. I've worked with dogs that had bite histories, dogs that were scheduled for euthanasia, dogs that had been through 5 trainers. They all improved. Some of them are now the best-behaved dogs I've ever trained.

3. The owners who get the best results are the ones who follow through. Training doesn't end when your dog comes home. It starts. The people who maintain the structure, use the tools, and stay consistent get permanent results. The ones who go back to old habits get old behaviour.

That's it. Fifteen years in three sentences.

If your dog is struggling and you're willing to do the work (or let us do it for you), reply to this email. I'll tell you exactly what I'd do.
