**Preview:** What your dog's day actually looks like at our facility.

---

Most people have never seen the inside of a board and train facility. Here's what ours looks like.

**Our Stouffville facility (22 Cardico Dr, Gormley):**

🏠 Purpose-built for dog training. Not a converted warehouse or someone's garage.

🌿 Open fields for off-leash recall work and e-collar conditioning.

🐕 Supervised socialization areas where dogs interact in structured, controlled groups.

🛣️ Access to trails, parks, and real-world environments for proofing.

**What a typical day looks like for a board and train dog:**

6:30 AM — Wake up, morning walk, leash work
9:00 AM — Structured training (obedience, commands, behaviour work)
11:00 AM — Socialization and supervised play
1:00 PM — Crate rest and settling practice
2:30 PM — Real-world proofing (outdoor environments, distractions)
4:00 PM — Play time, enrichment
6:00 PM — Evening walk, calm settling
8:00 PM — Bedtime routine

That's a full day. Every day. For 2 to 8 weeks.

**This is also where our daycare and boarding programs run.** If your dog has graduated from Board & Train and you need daycare or boarding, they already know the place and the team.
