**Preview:** The training window closes faster than you think.

If your puppy is under 5.5 months old, you have a window right now that closes fast.

The socialization period ends around 14-16 weeks. What your puppy learns (or doesn't learn) during this time shapes their entire adult personality. Miss it, and you spend years trying to fix what could have been prevented in weeks.
