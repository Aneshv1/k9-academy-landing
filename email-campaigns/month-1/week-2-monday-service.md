## What Your Dog's Day Looks Like at K9 Academy

Most board and train programs are glorified boarding with 20 minutes of training. Here's what ours actually looks like:

**Morning:**
- Structured walk and leash work
- Obedience drills (sit, down, stay, place, recall)
- E-collar conditioning and communication

**Midday:**
- Supervised socialization with other dogs
- Play time and structured interaction
- Crate training and settling practice

**Afternoon:**
- Real-world proofing (parks, streets, distractions)
- Advanced obedience under pressure
- Outdoor experiences and environmental exposure

**Evening:**
- Calm settling practice
- Structured routine before bed

That's 3-4 hours of training plus multiple hours of socialization and play. Every single day.

**Your dog also gets:**
- Weekly photo and video updates
- Handler training sessions before pickup
- Lifetime access to group classes and drop-ins after graduation
