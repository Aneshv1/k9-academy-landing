**Subject:** Your dog pulls because you taught them to

**Preview:** (leave blank for plain-text)

---

Nobody trains their dog to pull on leash on purpose. But almost every owner does it by accident.

Here's how it happens.

Your dog pulls forward. You keep walking. Your dog learns that pulling = moving forward = reward.

You just trained your dog to pull. A thousand times.

The fix is simple but painful for the first few days. When your dog pulls, stop. Completely. Don't move until the leash is slack. Then walk. When they pull again, stop again.

Most dogs figure this out by day 4 or 5. The first two days feel impossible. That's normal.
