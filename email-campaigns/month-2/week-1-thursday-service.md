**Subject:** Why 3 stays beats 1 for your puppy

**Preview:** The Puppy Start Right program hits every critical window. Here's how.

Most puppy programs give you one round of training and send you home. The problem? Your puppy's brain keeps developing for the next 12 months. What works at 10 weeks won't cut it at 6 months.

**Puppy Start Right ($3,995): 3 board and train stays across your puppy's first year.**

**Stay 1 (8-14 weeks):** Socialization, bite inhibition, crate training, house manners. This is the critical window that closes fast. We get your puppy exposed to everything they need to be confident for life.

**Stay 2 (5-6 months):** Adolescence hits. Boundaries get tested. We layer in obedience, impulse control, and leash manners before bad habits take root.

**Stay 3 (9-12 months):** Real-world proofing. Off-leash work. E-collar conditioning. Your dog graduates as a trained adult, not a teenager who forgot everything.

**One stay gives you a foundation. Three stays give you a finished dog.**

The difference is massive. Puppies who go through all three stages skip the "difficult adolescent phase" that most owners dread.
