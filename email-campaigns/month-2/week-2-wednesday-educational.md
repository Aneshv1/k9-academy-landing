**Subject:** How to stop your puppy biting in 3 days

**Preview:** It's not aggression. But you need to handle it now.

---

Every puppy bites. It's normal. But if you don't address it early, those needle teeth turn into a real problem at 40, 60, or 80 pounds.

Here's the 3-day protocol we use at K9 Academy:

**Day 1: Stop rewarding the bite.**
When your puppy bites, stand up immediately. Turn away. Leave the room for 10 seconds. Come back. Repeat. Every single time. No yelling, no pushing them away. That's engagement and engagement is a reward.

**Day 2: Redirect before the bite.**
Watch for the moment right before the bite. Ears forward, mouth opening, lunging at your hand. That's when you put a toy in their mouth instead. You're teaching them what TO bite, not just what not to bite.

**Day 3: Reward calm mouth contact.**
When your puppy licks your hand instead of biting, mark it. "Yes." Treat. When they chew a toy near you instead of your sleeve, mark it. "Yes." Treat.

**The pattern: remove attention for biting, redirect to appropriate items, reward the behavior you want.**

Three days of consistency and most puppies cut the biting by 80%. The remaining 20% takes another week.
